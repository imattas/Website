## Exploitation / Solution

Source: [forensics/Are-You-Looking-Me-Up.md](https://github.com/BYU-CSA/BYUCTF-2025/blob/main/forensics/Are-You-Looking-Me-Up.md)

Look for which devices have a lot of UDP port 53 traffic:

```bash
cat logs.txt | grep udp | awk -F',' '$22 == 53 {print $20}' | sort | uniq -c | sort -nr | head
```
